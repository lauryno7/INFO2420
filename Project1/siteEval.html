Web Site Critiques

Trader Joe's - www.traderjoes.com

Questions

  1. What was your first impression of this site?

    2. I think the site is so fun! There are very cute colors, pictures, fonts, etc. The layout as a whole is easy to read and navigate. The inclusion of specific recipes and new product info is very helpful.

  2. How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they have links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)

    3. The site does have a few links on the left, but there is also a main drop down menu at the top left. If you scroll down the home page, there is also a number of links to different sections of the site. The layout is very organized and easy to follow.

  3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?

    4. The site structure mostly makes sense. I think it could be a little better honestly, maybe with a few more links/sections on the left side, or a drop down that is more intuitive on the home page.

  4. Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?

    5. I think it enhances it. Trader Joe's is known for their cute art and designs around their stores and on their packaging, and I think they've done a great job of incorporating that into their website. It's not too much by any means, in my opinion.

  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?

    6. The load time was very reasonable. It only took a second or two at most.

  6. Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?

    7. Yes, it was very readable. A background image was not used, and the site itself looks to be an off-white color which keeps it simple and clean looking.

  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?

    8. It was pretty well done, I think. As mentioned before, I think it could be better. But all things considered, it's pretty good. There just seems to be more clicking than necessary to get to certain sections.

  8. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?

    9. Yes, the same color scheme was used across the board. Each page featured similar colors, fonts, and layouts. The only thing that changed was the content of the page itself.

  9. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?

    10. It responds quite well. I shrunk the page and made it larger, skinnier, fatter, etc. and the site adjusted very well.

  10. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

    11. I think the incorporation of the recipes was very well done. It made the site feel cute and personal. There were different options using many of their products which I appreciate about this site a lot. It seems to appeal to a lot of different types of customers.
